Rainy Voxel World

Small Challenge: Pick the items in the buildings.
Step in any direction you like, there is no special order: every platform and object is clickable in a certain way. Find the items (which are four) and they will disappear.

Supports teleporting. So, lean back and dive in.

Notes

Working with Magica Voxel objects requires export as .glb or .obj and .mtl. Used here is the latter method. They had to be refined in Blender: first, remesh the object and decimate as much polygons/triangles as possible.

Then, bake the texture of the original on a duplicate of the object. Make sure to save the palette.png in the same folder. Helpful video on YouTube: MagicaVoxel - Optimizing and exporting of 3D voxel models.

In the Rainy Voxel World I rotated the objects in non-right y-angles which makes them look a bit jagged and pixelated. But I'm OK with that appearance.
